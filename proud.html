Meet the Bit Press

I was tasked with designing and implementing the electronics and logic of a custom machine. The purpose of the machine is to press the drive end and the drill bit end together and then weld them using a laser.

The Mechanics

Here you can see the rails, air cyclinders, tubes, wireing and switches. This is where all the motion happens. This chamber is wired for interlocks and fail-safes to keep the operator safe.

The Brains

Here you can see the PLC, motor drivers, solenoids, fuses and wiring. Each component had to be selected to work with the others.
